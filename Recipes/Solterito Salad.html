Solterito Salad

★★★★

Appetizer, Beans, Peruvian, Sides

Prep Time: 15 mins Cook Time: 15 mins Servings: 2 Source: perudelights.com

Ingredients

1 cup fava beans

½ cup red onion

1 cup tomato

1 cup giant kernel corn

1 cup queso freso

¼ rocoto (Peruvian red chili pepper)

3 tablespoons red or white wine vinegar

3 tablespoons olive oil

2 tablespoons chopped parsley

Directions

Cook beans in boiling salted water for 5 minutes. Strain and peel.

Cook giant kernel corn

Dice the onion, tomato, cheese and peppers into small pieces

In a bowl combine all the ingredients and season with vinegar, olive oil, salt and pepper.

Add the parsley.
