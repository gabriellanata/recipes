Caesar Salad

★★★★

Salads, Sides, Small Dishes

Prep Time: 20 min Cook Time: 10 min Total Time: 30 min Difficulty: Easy Servings: 2 large salads Source: onceuponachef.com

Ingredients

0.7 lb chicken breast

2 garlic cloves, minced

1 teaspoon anchovy paste (found near the canned tuna in the supermarket)

2 tablespoons lemon juice

1 teaspoon Dijon mustard

1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce

1 cup mayonnaise

1/2 cup freshly grated Parmigiano-Reggiano

1/4 teaspoon salt

1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper

1 piece of bread for croutons

Directions

In a medium bowl, whisk together the garlic, anchovy paste, lemon juice, Dijon mustard and Worcestershire sauce. Add the mayonnaise, Parmigiano-Reggiano, salt and pepper and whisk until well combined. Taste and adjust to your liking. The dressing will keep well in the fridge for about a week.
