Burrito Bowl

★★★★

Add To Meal Plan, Mexican, Vegetarian

Prep Time: 10 minutes Cook Time: 20 minutes Servings: Serving size: 4 servings Source: foodwithfeeling.com

Ingredients

1 cup corn

1 cup rice

1 cup of cilantro

3 limes

1/2 bell pepper

1/4 red onion

1 tbsp of oil

1/2 teaspoon of cumin

1/2 teaspoon of garlic powder

1/2 teaspoon of paprika

1 can of black beans

1 cup of thinly sliced leaf lettuce

1/2 cup sour cream

1 avocado

1/2 cup mexican cheese

pico de gallo?

tortilla chips?

Directions

To make the rice: add rice and water to small saucepan and bring to a boil. Add the zest of one lime and a pinch of salt. Cook on low heat for 15 minutes. Once done, add tons of cilantro the juice of the lime.

Use a small frying pan to sautee the peppers and onions in olive oil for 10 minutes. Add the spices here.

Make guacamole by mashing the avocado and mixing it with lime juice, salt, and any other spices.

Once everything is almost done, warm the corn and black beans in the microwave for 45 seconds.

Assemble the bowls by first dividing the rice among the bowls, top with the veggies then the black beans and then sprinkle on the corn and other toppings (guacamole, sour cream, cheese).
