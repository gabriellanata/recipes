Pan Pizza

★★★★★

Vegetarian

Difficulty: Medium Servings: 2 Source: Javier Saez

Ingredients

1 bag Trader Joe’s Pizza dough

1/3 cup Pizza sauce

8 ounces shredded mozzarella cheese

1 Burrata cheese ball

Arugala

Fresh Basil

Salami

Directions

Mix the flour with oregano and a pinch of salt.

Slowly add warm water until you get a soft dough (barely not sticky), form a ball. If the dough is too sticky, add more flour.

Coat the outside of the dough ball with olive oil.

On a cold pan, add olive oil and stretch the dough.

Cook first side at high heat to seal the dough quickly. Then turn and cook at slow heat until golden.

Turn over one last time, add the cheese and ingredients, cook in slow heat to avoid burning until cheese has melted slightly. If using slow cooked ingredients (sausage or meat) its better to pre cook them separately.
