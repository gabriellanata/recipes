Sour Parsley Pasta

★★★★★

Pasta, Vegetarian

Ingredients

Spaghetti for 2

Oil for sautee

3 cloves garlic minced

1/2 cup white vinegar

Lots of parsley flaked

Red pepper flaked

Black pepper

Garlic powder

Powdered parmesean

2 eggs

Directions

1. Make pasta in generously salted water
2. While pasta is cooking, heat a large skillet and sautee garlic (2 finely minced, 1 roughly chopped).
3. Add red pepper flakes.
4. Add vinegar.
5. In a separate bowl, beat the eggs and add powder parmesean generously. Add salt.
6. Add 1/4 cup pasta water to egg mix.
7. Drain pasta and add to skillet.
8. Once mixed, move the pan away from the heat and work in the egg mixture.
9. Add salt, pepper, garlic powder, and lots of psrsley.
