Arroz Chaufa WIth Quinoa

★★★★

Peruvian, Rice

Prep Time: 15 mins Cook Time: 10 mins Difficulty: Easy Servings: 2 Source: whiskitrealgud.com

Ingredients

0.8 pounds chicken

5 eggs

1/2 cup white rice

1/2 cup quinoa

1/4 white onion

2 garlic cloves

1 whole red bell pepper

1/2 cup green onions

2 tbsp vegetable oil

1/8 cup soy sauce

1/4 tsp sesame oil

2 pinches cumin

2 tablespoons grated ginger

1/4 tsp ground ginger

1 pinch of sugar

Directions

Preparation:

Cook white rice with half chicken broth or bullion instead of water. Ideally wait until cold and slightly dry.

Cook quinoa.

Make scrambled eggs.

Dice chicken into 1/2 inch cubes.

Dice bell pepper and onion small. Mince garlic. Grate fresh ginger.

Cut green onion small, greens and whites included.

Make soy sauce mixture by mixing: Soy sauce, sesame oil, a pinch or two of sugar, couple pinches of cumin, ground ginger.

Cooking:

Heat the oil in a large skillet over medium high heat.

Add the red bell peppers and onions. Sauté until softened, about 6 minutes.

Add green onions, fresh ginger and chicken. Cook for a couple minutes or until cooked through.

Add the rice. Mix well then add the quinoa. Mix well.

Add the soy sauce mixture. Mix well then add the chopped scrambled eggs.

Toss. Season with salt and pepper. Add MSG to make it taste great.

Notes

Preparations tips:

This recipe moves pretty fast so get all of your veggies diced and minces and in order.

Skinless chicken thighs is the preferred choice. They are full of flavor and they will taste amazing in this dish. If all you have is chicken breast, feel free do use that too.
