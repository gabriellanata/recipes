Grilled Halloumi Greek Flatbreads

★★★★★

Add To Meal Plan, Mediteranian, Vegetarian

Servings: Serving: 1 Source: hungryhealthyhappy.com

Ingredients

250 g Halloumi

2 Greek pitas

1/2 container of hummus

1/2 container of tzatziki

2 cups arrugula

1/4 red pepper

1/4 red onion

1/4 cucumber

1/4 cup artichoke hearts

4 kalamata olives

Fresh oregano

Fresh parsley

Directions

Finely chop all of the vegetables.

Slice the halloumi about 1cm thick.

Add a little oil to a griddle pan and heat on low. Put the halloumi on to a griddle pan and cook for 2 minutes each side.

Microwave the pitas 30 seconds before preparing.

Spread the flatbread with hummus and tzatziki then fill with chopped vegetables and cheese.

Notes

The best way to cook halloumi is either on a griddle pan or out on the BBQ, as it cooks really quickly and you get those nice grill lines on the slices of cheese.

For a true Greek street food flatbread, add a handful of fries to the filling.

Other filling ideas are chicken, lamb souvlaki or falafel.
