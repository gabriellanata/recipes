Filipino Adobo Chicken

★★★★

Asian, Poultry

Cook Time: 1 hour Servings: 2 Source: skinnytaste.com

Ingredients

1 chicken breast

1/3 cup soy sauce

1/3 cup apple cider vinegar

3 cloves garlic

6 ground peppercorns

4 bay leaves

1 jalapeño, chopped

1/2 cup green onions

Directions

Cut chicken into square pieces.

Marinate chicken in vinegar, soy sauce, bay leaves, garlic, jalapeño and pepper, for at least an hour (overnight is ideal).

Put marinade into a deep nonstick skillet and cook on medium-low heat. Cover and cook until the meat is tender, about 45 minutes.

Remove the cover and cook an additional 15 minutes, until the sauce reduces.

Discard bay leaves and serve over rice if you wish.

Top with green onions
