Lentils with Italian Sausage

★★★★★

Beans, Leftover Friendly, Peruvian

Prep Time: 30 minutes Cook Time: 1 hour and a half Total Time: 2 hours Difficulty: Medium Servings: 2 Source: cookingwithnonna.com

Ingredients

2/3 cup lentils

1 Italian sausage links

1/4 large onion

2 cloves of garlic

parsley

olive oil

Directions

In the largest frying pan, heat 1 Tbsp olive oil on high heat.

Cook the sausages until blackened.

While the sausages cook, dice the onion and garlic (can use food processor).

Let sausages cool for a minute. Cut one of the sausages into chunks, and pulverize the other in the food processor.

Sautee the onion and garlic with olive oil on medium heat until transarant.

Add the sausages and cook for another minute.

Add the lentils (1/3 cup per person) and season with salt, pepper, and garlic.

Add water and cover. Cook for 30 minutes or until done.

At end add the parsley.

Serve by adding a dash of olive oil.
