Baked Broccoli Macaroni and Cheese

★★★★

Pasta, Vegetarian

Prep Time: 10 mins Cook Time: 25 mins Servings: Yield: 8 Source: skinnytaste.com

Ingredients

Elbow pasta for 2 people (4.5 oz)

1 1/2 tbsp butter

1/4 cup yellow onion

1/4 cup flour

1 cup milk

1 cup chicken broth

2 cups sharp cheddar cheese

6 oz broccoli

1/4 cup parmesan

1/4 cup seasoned bread crumbs

Directions

Preheat oven to 375°F.

Melt butter in a deep saucepan.

Chop the onion and start boiling water.

Add onion and cook over low heat about 2 minutes.

Cook the pasta for 8 minutes. Be sure to add the broccoli for the last two minutes!!

Add flour and cook another minute, or until the flour is golden and well combined.

Add milk and chicken broth and whisk, raising heat to medium-high until it comes to a boil; cook about 5 minutes or until the sauce becomes smooth and thick.

Season with salt and pepper.

Once the sauce is thick, remove from heat, add cheese and mix well until cheese is melted. Adjust salt and pepper to taste.

Add cooked macaroni and broccoli and mix well.

Pour into prepared baking dish. Top with grated cheese and breadcrumbs. Spray a little more cooking spray on top.

Bake for 15 minutes.
