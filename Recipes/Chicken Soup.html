Chicken Soup

★★★★

Leftover Friendly, Poultry, Soup

Ingredients

1-2 chicken breasts with skin on, or a whole chicken

Oil for coating the chicken

Salt, pepper, garlic for seasoning chicken

3 carrots

3 celery ribs

5 cloves garlic

1/2 onion

1 cup white rice

Directions

1. Coat chicken in oil and seasoning and bake in a pan until crispy.
2. Chop veggies into itty bitty pieces.

If using the instant pot, you can sautee the veggies in there until semi-soft and then add chicken and juices. Cook for 2 hours.

If using regular stove, do the same as above and cook until you feel like it. 2-4 hours.

While soup is cooking, make white rice. 1:1 rice to water.

Once complete, shred one of the chicken breasts and add to soup.

Season with salt and peoper generously.

Add rice to soup.

Enjoy with lots of lemon!

IF FREEZING SOUP DO NOT ADD RICE
