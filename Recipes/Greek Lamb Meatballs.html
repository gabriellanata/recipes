Greek Lamb Meatballs

★★★★

Guest Menu, Lamb, Leftover Friendly

Prep Time: 25 mins Cook Time: 13 mins Servings: 4 Source: paleocupboard.com

Ingredients

1 lb. ground beef/ lamb

1/2 onion

3 garlic cloves

1 lemon (juice and zest)

1 Tbsp. dried parsley

1 Tbsp. sea salt

1 Tbsp. onion powder

1/4 tsp. cinnamon

1 Tbsp. cumin

1 Tbsp. dried oregano

1 Tbsp. ground black pepper

1/4 cup fresh parsley

1 large egg

Tzatziki Sauce

Directions

Place all ingredients in a large mixing bowl and mix until well combined. Should form 14 meatballs (2 servings for 2 people).

Heat butter and olive oil in pan on high (9).

Carefully place each meatball in the pan, moving to brown them on all sides.

If using the oven, bake on for 13 minutes at 425 degrees.

Serve with the Tzatziki Sauce and Mumma Rice.
