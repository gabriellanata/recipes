Asian Stir Fry

★★★★★

Asian

Servings: Yield: 2 Source: pinchofyum.com

Ingredients

2 tbsp sesame oil

sesame seeds for garnish

1/4 cup rice vinegar

1/2 cup vegetable oil for frying

1/2 cup coconut aminos

1 cup broccoli

2 carrots

1/2 bell pepper

1/4 cup frozen peas

4 cloves garlic

1/4 onion

1 chicken breast

1 cup flour

1/3 cup cornstarch

3 eggs

1 pack asian noodles

Directions

1. Sautee the diced vegetables in sesame oil. (Not including the peas).
2. Dice the chicken and coat in the egg before coating in the flour/cornstarch mixture.
3. In another pan, fry the chicken in a combo of mostly vegetable oil and a lil sesame oil.
4. Once brown on both sides, let chicken dry on a paper towel.
5. Boil water and cook the noodles until tender.
6. While the noodles are cooking, make scrambled eggs on one side of the pan. Then add the peas.
7. Combine everything and add a generous amount of coconut aminos. Add extra sesame oil and vinegar if needed.
8. Add hot flakes or paste.
9. Garnish with scallions and sesame seeds.
