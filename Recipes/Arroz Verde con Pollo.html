Arroz Verde con Pollo

★★★★

Peruvian, Poultry, Rice

Ingredients

1/2 yellow onion

2 carrots

4 garlic cloves

1/4 red pepper

1/4 cup peas

3/4lb chicken

3/4 cup rice

3/4 cup chicken broth

1/2 packet cilantro powder

Directions

Get rice ingredients ready with slightly less water than usual.

Dice the onion, garlic, red pepper, and carrots.

Sautee vegetables in oil on low heat.

Chop chicken into small pieces. Season with salt, garlic, peoper, and paprika.

Add chicken and rice to pan.

Stir everything and add water/chicken broth, and powder.

Cover and bring to a boil. Then lower to low heat (3) and cook for 15 minutes.

Add peas.

Serve with cilantro.


Notes

Can also be enjoyed with Peruvian sauces!
