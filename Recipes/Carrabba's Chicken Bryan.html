Carrabba's Chicken Bryan

★★★★

Poultry

Prep Time: 35 min Cook Time: 55mins Servings: Serves: 2 Source: geniuskitchen.com

Ingredients

4 garlic cloves

1/4 minced yellow onion

2 tablespoons butter

1/2 cup dry white wine

1/4 cup fresh lemon juice

2/3 cup cold butter

1/4 cup chopped sun-dried tomatoes

1/3 cup chopped fresh basil

1/2 teaspoon salt

1 chicken breast

1/2 teaspoon salt

1/2 teaspoon black pepper

Sprinkle of goat cheese

Directions

Saute garlic and onion in 2 tbsp. butter in a large skillet over medium heat until tender.

Stir wine and lemon juice into skillet, increase heat to medium high, and simmer to reduce by half.

Reduce heat to low and stir in cold butter, one slice at a time.

Stir in tomatoes, basil, salt, and pepper; remove from heat; set aside.

Cut chicken breasts into cubes and season.

In small frying pan, sautee chicken on high heat (9) for 3 minutes. Then flip chicken and cook for 3 more min.

Once chicken is done, mix in with the sauce. Add goat cheese crumbles on top before serving.
