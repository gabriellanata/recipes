Vegetarian Power Bowls

★★★★★

Vegetarian

Prep Time: 15 mins Cook Time: 40 mins Difficulty: Hard Servings: 2 Source: wellplated.com

Ingredients

3/4 cup brown rice

3 eggs


1/2 red onion

1/2 sweet potato

1 head broccoli

1 head cauliflower

2 beets

1 bunch of kale

1 tbsp salt

1 tbsp black pepper

1 tbsp paprika

1 tbsp garlic powder




3 tablespoons lemon juice

3 tablespoons tahini

1 clove garlic

1 teaspoon cumin

1/4 teaspoon salt

Directions

Preheat oven to 400 degrees.

Make brown rice in instant pot. (1 cup rice: 1 cup water). Cook for 15 min, followed by 10 min release. Season w/salt and olive oil when done.

Dice sweet potato, red onion, broccoli, cauliflower, and beets into bite-sized pieces and place on sheet pan. (Tip: cut beets last since they stain).

Coat vegetables in olive oil and all spices. Mix.

Cook until tender, about 30-45 min. Add kale on top for the last 5 min.

Make and peel the hard boiled eggs. Once done, soak them in soy sauce.

Prepare the sauce by combining all ingredients and adding water to thin if needed.

Serve in a bowl, with rice on bottom, followed by vegetables and eggs.

Notes

To cook hardboiled eggs: you can make these in your Instant Pot (this is a great tutorial) or on your stove by following the directions in step 3 of this post.

Leftovers: Store roasted vegetables in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 4 days. Store dressing in a jar in the refrigerator and eggs in the refrigerator for up to 1 week. Reheat the vegetables gently in the microwave, and then top with the egg and dressing just before serving.

This recipe is ultra flexible. Feel free to swap out any of the roasted veggies for others you like. Brussels sprouts are another of my favorite additions.
